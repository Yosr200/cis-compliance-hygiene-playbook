# CIS Compliance KPIs (Operational + Governance)

## Core KPIs
- Compliance coverage (% assets assessed)
- Compliance posture (% passing)
- High-risk deviation count (Tier 0/1)
- Mean time to remediate (MTTR) for compliance items
- SLA adherence (% closed within SLA)
- Exceptions count + aging (accepted risks)

## How to use KPIs
- Operations: drive remediation actions
- Governance: confirm risk appetite alignment and resource needs
